# CI/CD Security (SAST/DAST/SCA Integration) — Mid-Senior

1. How do you integrate SAST without blocking developers? — Triage rules and progressive enforcement.
2. How do you handle false positives at scale? — Baselines and risk-based suppression.
3. How do you secure CI runners? — Ephemeral runners, minimal privileges.
4. How do you prevent dependency confusion? — Private registries and namespace controls.
5. How do you manage secrets in pipelines? — Use vaults and short-lived tokens.
6. How do you ensure artifact integrity? — Sign and verify in deployment.
7. How do you implement DAST in CI? — Use staging envs and test accounts.
8. How do you validate container images? — Scan layers and enforce policies.
9. How do you manage SBOMs? — Generate per build and store in artifacts.
10. How do you prevent supply-chain tampering in CI? — Protected branches and verified actions.
11. How do you handle third-party CI actions? — Pin versions and allowlist.
12. How do you integrate IaC scanning? — Terraform/K8s policy checks.
13. How do you handle monorepo scanning? — Path-based rules and incremental scans.
14. How do you limit secrets exposure in logs? — Masking and no-echo settings.
15. How do you ensure build reproducibility? — Locked deps and hermetic builds.
